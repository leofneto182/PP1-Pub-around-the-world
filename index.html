Have you ever been on a Pub Crawl?

Let's get to know it!

How does it work?

A pub crawl (sometimes called a bar tour, bar crawl or bar-hopping) is the act of visiting multiple pubs or bars in a single session.

Is there a specific date?

Although some countries have the tradition of holding the Pub Crawl on specific dates, you can also go on it to celebrate a birthday, a bachelor party or even on a random day just to go out with friends and meet new people.

Put your phone down!

Pub crawling is about meeting new people in new places and enjoying shared experiences, as well as getting out of comfort zone. Put down the phone, fun is happening right in front of you!
